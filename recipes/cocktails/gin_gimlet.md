# Gin Gimlet

## Ingredients

- 1 oz Dry gin
- 1 oz Lime juice
- 1 oz Simple syrup

## Instructions

1. Combine gin, lime juice and simple syrup in cocktail shaker
2. Shake with ice
3. Strain and serve up

## Variations

- Substitute simple syrup with maple syrup
