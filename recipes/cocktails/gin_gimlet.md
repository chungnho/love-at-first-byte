# Gin Gimlet

## Ingredients

- 2 oz Dry gin
- 1 oz Lemon juice
- 1 oz Simple syrup
- Club soda to top

#### Garnish (optional)

- Lemon wheel
- Maraschino cherry

## Instructions

1. Combine gin, lemon juice and simple syrup (shake or stir)
2. Top with club soda
3. Garnish

## Variations

- Earl Grey Collins - Use Earl Grey infused gin (soak an earl grey tea bag in up to 500 mL of gin for ~8h)
