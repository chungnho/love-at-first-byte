# Espresso Martini

## Ingredients

- 2 oz Vodka
- 1 oz Espresso
- 1 oz Coffee liqueur (eg Kahlua)

### Garnishes (optional)

- Coffee beans

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in cocktail shaker
2. Shake with ice
3. Strain and serve up
4. Garnish with coffee beans

## Variations

- Substitute .5 oz coffee liqueur with syrup or other liqueurs (eg Baileys)
